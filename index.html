This year has been a journey filled with growth, challenges, and blessings. As a 3rd-year college student at Cebu Technological University Danao campus, I've been balancing academics, programming projects, and my involvement in UAPH Cebu. From organizing events to working on our school's map directory, every experience has shaped me into a better version of myself.

Despite the busy schedule, my CPE Boiz group and my special friend Gaylord have kept me motivated. There have been moments of struggle, but also victories worth celebrating. Looking ahead, I'm excited for what's to come—new opportunities, meaningful connections, and more lessons that will help me grow. 2024 is definitely a year of purpose and perseverance!
